The Process of
Designing A Website

Creating a website takes a lot of time and effort. 

You need to find a reliable hosting provider, then make sure you choose the right hosting plan,
then actually design the website (which is almost impossible to do if you don't already know 
how to code, especially if you are busy running your business), and then deploying it.

And that's ignoring the fact that your website might need to be hosted along side a database, 
that you will need to be able to query and read/write information to.

If you choose to hire me, I will take away those worries. 

My Approach

When you choose to hire me, I will keep things simple and make sure that you have as much control over the development proccess as you want, whether that means you want full control or to leave it up to me.

  • When we first talk, you will tell me what your business needs are, and I will recommend the right pack for you (although you will be free to choose which one you like). From there, I will get to work, making sure that I keep you as involved in the development process as you want to be.
  • I will begin sketching a mock up of the website, not fully commiting to one design, but setting out to plan the main strucutre of the website and its pages.
  • Once this is complete, I will consult with you to make sure you approve of the website structure.
  • If you require a copywriting service, ie, you need me to write the content for you website, I will then begin to write the content for the various pages on your website, making sure that the content is on brand and represents what your business stands for.
  • In the meantime, you can send any photos you want uploaded on your website. Or you can leave that to me, as I can produce high quality stock images for your website.
  • Once I have all the material I need for the website, I will begin to design your own professional website. Upon request, I will provide screen shots of the current the design to make sure you are happy with the direction the website is going in. 
  • Finally, once the website is completed, I will host the website for you, with your required domain name.

Hurray! 
Your website would now be up and running

Congratulations, after all our hard work together, you are finally the proud owner of your business website.  It's time for a celebration...

Your website will be available at the domain of your choosing, and will over time become indexed by a search engine like Google.
